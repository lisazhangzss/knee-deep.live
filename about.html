About the Game

Knee Deep! In Love is a yandere visual novel set at Pebblestone University. Players navigate campus life and romance while gradually uncovering that their closest companions are willing to kill for their affection.

Developed by Comfort Kuma Studios, the game features 25,000+ words of story content, partial voice acting, 6 hand-crafted CGs, and multiple endings. The demo is available for free on all platforms.
