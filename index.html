About Knee Deep! In Love

Welcome to Pebblestone University — where campus life seems perfectly ordinary until you notice the obsessive glances, the suspicious social media posts, and the way certain people always seem to know where you are. Knee Deep! In Love is a yandere visual novel with a cozy college aesthetic hiding genuine psychological horror.

Choose your pronouns, shape your story, and decide which of three love interests is a true sweetheart — and which would commit unspeakable acts for your affection. 25,000+ words, 6 CGs, and 2 distinct endings in the demo.
